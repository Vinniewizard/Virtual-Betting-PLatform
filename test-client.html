Blackjack Tutorial

How to Play Blackjack

  • Start a game by placing a bet and clicking Play Blackjack.
  • You are dealt two cards. The dealer also gets two cards (one face up).
  • Your goal: Get as close to 21 as possible without going over.
  • On your turn, you can Hit (draw a card), Stand (end your turn), Double (double your bet, draw one card, and stand), or Split (if your first two cards are the same rank, split into two hands).
  • Splitting creates two hands, each with its own bet. Play each hand separately.
  • After you finish, the dealer plays. If your hand beats the dealer, you win!
  • Blackjack pays 3:2. Standard win pays 1:1. If you bust (go over 21), you lose your bet.
  • Sound effects and card animations enhance your experience.

Tips

  • Double down when you have a strong starting hand (like 10 or 11).
  • Split pairs of Aces or 8s for best odds.
  • Use the tutorial and game guide for more info.
